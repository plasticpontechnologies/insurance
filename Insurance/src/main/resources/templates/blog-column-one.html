How to choose Insurance Plan

1. Term Plan

The death benefit from a term plan is only available for a specified period, 40 years from the date of policy purchase.Term insurance plan is a life insurance policy which offer you financial coverage in case of loss of life during the term. You pay a yearly premium against the policy to the insurance company.

2. Endowment Plan

Endowment plans are life insurance policies where a portion of your premiums go toward the death benefit, while the remaining is invested by the insurance provider. Maturity benefits, death benefit and periodic bonuses are some types of assistance from endowment policies.

3. Unit Linked Insurance Plans
